TOPICS IN THE INDUSTRY WORKSHOP
The Industry Workshop Topics focus on application-oriented discussions that bridge advanced technologies with real industrial needs. These workshops provide a collaborative platform for enterprises, solution providers, and researchers to share implementation experiences, discuss deployment challenges, and explore scalable and sustainable solutions across diverse industrial sectors. Emphasis is placed on practical use cases, technology adoption strategies, and industry-driven innovation that support intelligent, secure, and future-ready industrial ecosystems.
Quantum AI for Industrial Applications:
This topic highlights the transformative potential of AI and Quantum-inspired computing methods to tackle complex industrial challenges. Discussions will cover real-world applications of Quantum AI in optimizing supply chains, predictive maintenance, manufacturing planning, and data-driven decision-making. Emphasis will be placed on practical deployment strategies that help enterprises move from research prototypes to scalable industrial solutions.
Smart Chips & Intelligent Hardware Systems:
Focus here is on the design, development, and deployment of smart semiconductor technologies and hardware architectures that power AI acceleration, edge computing, and industrial automation. Topics include chip-level innovations for efficient AI processing, system-on-chip (SoC) integration for smart factory applications, and hardware-software co-design approaches that enable next-generation intelligent systems across sectors such as robotics, IoT, and smart manufacturing.
Cybersecurity & Secure Industrial Solutions:
Ensuring the security and trustworthiness of industrial systems is critical as digitalization deepens. This topic addresses cybersecurity strategies and technologies tailored for industrial environments, including secure-by-design practices, threat detection using AI, protection of edge devices and IoT networks, and compliance with evolving security standards. Discussions will focus on empowering enterprises to build resilient and secure digital operations.
