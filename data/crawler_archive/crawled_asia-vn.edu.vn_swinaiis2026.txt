Swinburne Industry Symposium 2026
The
Applied AI & Industry Intelligence Symposium
hosted by Swinburne Vietnam, is a forward-looking forum that brings together industry leaders, technology experts, academics, and businesses to explore how AI, data, and applied intelligence are transforming decision-making, operations, and competitiveness across industries.
The symposium focuses on the practical application of intelligence in real-world industry contexts, highlighting how organizations can leverage AI, data analytics, digital systems, and emerging technologies to optimize performance, enhance resilience, and drive sustainable growth. Through in-depth discussions and case-driven insights, the event aims to strengthen collaboration between academia and industry while fostering an ecosystem of applied innovation in Vietnam and the wider region.
