Register for the Conference "
The Future of Finance: Skills, Innovation & Career Success"
Organized jointly by
Swinburne Vietnam and CPA Australia
, this seminar brings together academics, industry representatives, and students to explore career pathways, future-ready skills, and international collaboration in Finance, Banking, Accounting, and Data Analytics.
Objectives:
Formalize and celebrate the partnership
between Swinburne Vietnam and CPA Australia through an official MOU signing.
Provide career guidance and professional insights
for students interested in Finance, Banking, Accounting, and Data Analytics careers.
Share knowledge on future workforce skills
, aligning academic preparation with industry demands.
Strengthen educational and professional collaboration
between Vietnam and Australia through shared initiatives.
Target Audiences:
CPA Australia members
Swinburne Vietnam students,
lecturers, faculty members
Invited industry guests
Event Details:
Time:
8:00 – 12:00 | Friday, January 23, 2026
Venue:
Swinburne Innovation Space,
No. 80 Duy Tan Street, Cau Giay District, Hanoi
Registration Deadline:
January 22, 2026
Contact for support:
conference@fe.edu.vn
